Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our OCR tool and its features.

❓ How does the Image to Text conversion work?
Our OCR tool uses AI-powered recognition to extract text from images with high accuracy. The process involves advanced preprocessing, text detection, and language modeling.
🌍 Which languages does the OCR tool support?
We support over 21+ languages, including English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Arabic, and many more.
🌙 Can I switch between Dark and Light mode?
Yes! Our website allows you to toggle between dark and light modes for an optimized user experience.
📂 What formats can I download my extracted text in?
You can download the extracted text as TXT, Word (DOCX), or Excel (XLSX) formats.
📄 Can I convert PDF files to images?
Yes! Our tool allows you to convert PDF documents into a ZIP file containing extracted images.
🌎 Can I use the website in different languages?
Yes, we support multiple language translations, including English, Hindi, Spanish, French, and German.
